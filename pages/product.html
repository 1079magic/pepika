About This Product

The Pepika Door Name Plate is precision-crafted using professional laser engraving on premium aluminium plate. Whether for a home, apartment block, office or business, it delivers a clean, modern look that lasts for years.

Each plate is made to order — we engrave your chosen name, family name, number or custom text with sharp, deep markings that won't fade or scratch off.

What's Included

  • 1 × Laser-engraved name plate (your size)
  • Mounting screws & wall anchors
  • Adhesive backing strip (optional fitting)
  • Protective packaging

Personalisation

When ordering, add your desired text in the order notes at checkout. We support up to 25 characters per line, 2 lines maximum. All common Latin characters, numbers and basic symbols are supported.
